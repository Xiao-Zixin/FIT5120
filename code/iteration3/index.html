Welcome to Queensland, Australia

Seeing a koala in Australia is a must-do! You can’t come half the way across the world and not seen one of these gorgeous and iconic animal.It’s very easy to see one in a zoo, but it’s far more fulfilling to see one in their natural environment.

   When is the best time of the year to spot Koalas?





Warmer months are the best time of the year to spot them. Koalas are most active at dawn and dusk. Sunset in summer is the best time to see koalas in the wild.

   How to spot wild koalas

Live on tree

Perched high in gum trees, with their stocky, large heads and fluffy ears. Koala’s often go unnoticed may appear to be little more than a bump on the tree itself.

Find them in the forks

The koala spends much of its time in the high forks of eucalyptus tree branches, so adjust your vision and hone in on the treetop hotspots to see koalas dozing in the trees

Take care

Koalas have been known to topple from trees. They usually survive falls and immediately climb back up, but injuries and deaths from falls do occur, particularly by speeding cars, so take care on the roads.
